about research

I am a graduate student at Stanford University in the Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics. I work in the Morphing Space Structures Lab, advised by Professor Manan Arya. My research is supported by the Stanford Graduate Fellowship in Science & Engineering.

Previously, I was a Fulbright Scholar at the Technische Universität Berlin in Germany, as a researcher in the Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics. Before that, I graduated from Caltech with a Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering with a minor in Geophysics. I have also completed internships at NASA, SpaceX, and others.
